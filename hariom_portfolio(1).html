About Me

Who I Am

I'm Hariom Gupta, an AI & Data Engineer currently pursuing my M.Tech in Information Technology (Data Engineering) at the prestigious Indian Institute of Information Technology, Allahabad, where I maintain a CGPA of 8.02.

I specialise in building intelligent, production-ready AI systems — from designing end-to-end RAG pipelines that run entirely locally, to engineering scalable big data architectures for real-time healthcare monitoring.

I'm deeply passionate about the intersection of large language models and practical software engineering. I believe AI should be private, fast, and grounded — which is why my work often focuses on local inference, offline pipelines, and systems that don't leak data or hallucinate answers.

Outside of research, I serve as a Teaching Assistant for Data Structures & Algorithms, mentoring 100+ B.Tech students through complex concepts. Having solved 350+ algorithmic problems myself, I bring both depth and clarity to every teaching session.
